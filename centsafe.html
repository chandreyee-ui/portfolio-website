Centsafe

Your trusted companion for effortless savings, helping you reach your financial goals with confidence and ease.

UX/UI

Mobile App Design

Fintech App

Centsafe is a mobile application designed to help users save money efficiently for significant purchases, such as weddings, vacations, or new cars. The app offers tools to track income and expenses, set and monitor savings goals.

Designed with simplicity and trustworthiness in mind, Centsafe aims to empower users who may not be financially savvy to take control of their savings journey.

Project overview

Role:

As the sole UX/UI Designer, I was responsible for conducting comprehensive user research, developing wireframes, and iterating on designs based on usability testing.

Tools

  • Figma
  • Adobe Photoshop

Duration:

July 18- August 10, 2024

Purpose and Context:

Centsafe was developed during my CareerFoundry course to address the challenge of quickly saving money for a specific goal. Targeted at users familiar with technology but uncomfortable managing finances, the goal was to simplify saving while boosting user confidence and control.

Objective:

In today’s fast-paced world, saving money for a big purchase can feel like trying to fill a leaky bucket. Enter Centsafe, a responsive web and mobile app designed to make saving as simple and stress-free as possible. Whether you're planning a dream vacation, buying a new car, or preparing for a wedding, Centsafe helps you take control of your finances, providing the tools and guidance needed to reach your savings goals with confidence. With a focus on clarity and trust, Centsafe transforms the daunting task of managing finances into an empowering experience.

What Went Well:

The development of Centsafe successfully translated user needs into a streamlined, user-friendly financial tool. The clean design, combined with intuitive features like Auto-Save, received positive feedback for its simplicity and effectiveness in helping users reach their savings goals.

What Didn't Go Well:

In the early stages, balancing the app's simplicity with the inclusion of advanced features proved difficult. Some initial designs were too complex for the target audience, requiring multiple revisions to achieve the right level of clarity and accessibility. Additionally, the time constraints limited the depth of analytics and educational features that could be implemented.

Key Learnings:

The key takeaway for me was- designing for simplicity without sacrificing functionality is crucial, especially for users who may be less familiar with financial tools. I learned that iterative testing and user feedback are essential to refining the user experience and ensuring the app meets users' needs. Additionally, clear communication of financial concepts through intuitive design is vital in building user confidence and trust.

If I Had More Time:

If I were to revisit this project, I would explore ways to enhance the app’s educational aspects, perhaps by incorporating more interactive elements that explain financial concepts in simple terms. Additionally, I see potential in expanding the app’s analytics features to offer predictive insights, helping users anticipate financial trends and adjust their savings strategies accordingly.
